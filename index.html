Vision 19 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Last Updated: October 2025

Vision 19 (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) values your trust and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information when you use any mobile application, website, or digital product developed or published by Vision 19 (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Scope

This policy applies to all Vision 19 applications and online properties, including future products released under the Vision 19 brand. It governs information collected from users worldwide, regardless of device or platform.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account registration data (name, email address, password)
  • Profile or preference information voluntarily entered
  • Support requests, feedback, or survey responses
  • Payment or subscription information processed through secure third-party vendors

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers (model, OS version, app version)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage analytics such as screens visited and features accessed
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

C. Information from Third Parties

If you sign in or link accounts (e.g., Apple, Google, Facebook), we may receive limited profile data as permitted by that provider’s terms.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the Services
  • Improve functionality, stability, and user experience
  • Communicate updates and respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse
  • Conduct internal analytics in aggregated, non-identifiable form

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS), role-based internal access, and ongoing vulnerability reviews. No online system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our protections.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us immediately for deletion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, withdraw consent, or opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@vision19.com.

7. International Data Transfers

Data may be stored or processed in multiple countries, including the U.S. We implement standard contractual clauses and safeguards to protect your information internationally.

8. Data Disclosure

We may disclose data to comply with legal requests, protect our rights or safety, or in connection with mergers or acquisitions, under confidentiality protections. We never disclose user data for advertising or profiling without consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will appear here with a revised “Last Updated” date. Major changes will be communicated in-app or via our website.
