Vision 19 Privacy Policy

Effective: Oct 2025  •  Last Updated: Oct 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile apps, websites, and services operated by Vision 19 (“Vision 19,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). By using our services, you agree to this Policy.

1) Scope and Who We Are

Vision 19 provides consumer software and related services (“Services”). This Policy covers information processed when you use any Vision 19 app or website, contact support, or otherwise interact with us. It applies worldwide and is designed to align with major privacy frameworks including GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, and PIPEDA.

Plain English: One company policy for all current and future Vision 19 apps; app-specific notices may appear inside each product.

2) Key Definitions

  • Personal Data: Information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to an individual.
  • Processing: Any operation on data (collecting, storing, using, sharing, deleting, etc.).
  • Controller: Vision 19, determining purposes and means of processing for our Services.
  • Processor: A third party that processes data on our behalf under contract.

3) Information We Collect

A. You Provide

  • Account data: name, email, password or sign-in token
  • Profile/preferences you enter in-app
  • Support requests, attachments, feedback, surveys
  • Billing identifiers handled by App Store / Play Billing (we do not receive full card details)

B. Collected Automatically

  • Device info: model, OS, app version, language, time zone
  • Approximate IP-based location, country/region
  • Usage analytics: screens, taps, session duration, feature adoption
  • Diagnostics: crash logs, performance and network error data

C. From Third Parties (if you opt in)

  • Sign-in providers (e.g., Apple): non-sensitive profile claims permitted by you
  • Cloud sync/backup vendors: content you choose to store with them

4) Why We Use Your Data

  • Provide core functionality, personalization, and account management
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse and fraud
  • Measure performance, quality, and stability; improve features
  • Communicate service messages and support responses
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms

We do not sell or rent personal data.

5) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract: to deliver the Services you request
  • Legitimate Interests: to run a safe, efficient service (balanced against your rights)
  • Consent: for optional analytics/marketing or platform-required permissions
  • Legal Obligation: to meet record-keeping and compliance duties

We keep Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or irreversibly anonymize it.

7) Security

  • TLS encryption in transit; hardened cloud infrastructure
  • Role-based access; least-privilege and audit logging
  • Secure software lifecycle: code review, dependency scanning, patching
  • Incident response processes aligned with industry best practices

No system is perfectly secure; we continuously improve safeguards.

8) When We Share Data

  • Processors under contract (hosting, analytics, error reporting, email delivery)
  • Legal: to comply with lawful requests or protect rights/safety
  • Corporate events: merger, acquisition, or asset transfer with confidentiality protections

We do not disclose data for third-party advertising or to data brokers.

9) Tracking, Analytics & Advertising

Our apps do not track users across other companies’ apps or websites for targeted advertising. If any app introduces cross-context tracking in the future, we will present platform-required consent (e.g., Apple’s AppTrackingTransparency) and honor your choice.

10) International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries with different laws (including the United States). Where required, we rely on adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses and implement supplemental safeguards.

11) Your Privacy Rights

Global

  • Access, correction, deletion
  • Portability (where applicable)
  • Withdraw consent without affecting prior lawful processing
  • Object or restrict certain processing

EEA/UK

You may contact our privacy team or your local authority. If we rely on legitimate interests, you can object where your rights outweigh our interests.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know categories/sources, purposes, and disclosures
  • Right to delete (subject to exceptions)
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” (we do not sell)
  • No retaliation for exercising rights

To exercise rights, email privacy@vision19.com. We may verify your request.

12) Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child provided data, contact us to delete it.

13) Do Not Track & Automated Decisions

Browsers may send “Do Not Track” signals; we do not respond to DNT at this time. We do not engage in automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without human involvement.

14) Incident Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your Personal Data, we will notify you and regulators as required by law and take steps to mitigate harm.
